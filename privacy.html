Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

MeetMe helps groups find a common meeting time. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide this service and never sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Email address — used to identify you and send magic-link sign-in emails and meeting invitations.
  • Display name — shown to other meeting participants.
  • Timezone — used to display availability in your local time.
  • Availability slots — the times you mark as available within a meeting.
  • Google Calendar (optional) — if you connect Google Calendar, we read free/busy data only; we never read event titles or descriptions.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via magic link or Google OAuth.
  • To display your name and availability to other meeting participants.
  • To send meeting invitations and reminder emails on your behalf.
  • To show aggregated availability across all participants.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Netlify infrastructure. Google Calendar OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. Session tokens are signed JWTs stored in HttpOnly cookies and are never accessible to JavaScript.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Resend (email delivery service) — receives your email address to deliver messages you request.
  • Google — if you connect Google Calendar, OAuth tokens are exchanged with Google's servers.

Data Retention

  • Active meeting data is retained as long as the meeting exists.
  • Anonymous meetings are automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity.
  • You may delete your meetings or leave meetings you were invited to at any time.
  • You may delete your account at any time from the app's Profile screen.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@meetme.pisan.me.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@meetme.pisan.me.
