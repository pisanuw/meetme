How it works

1

Create a meeting

Give your meeting a title, choose the days or dates to consider, and set the time window.

2

Share the invite link

Send the participation link to everyone you want to invite. They tap it, enter their name, and mark when they're available.

3

See who's free

Watch the heatmap fill in as responses come in. The best times stand out instantly.

4

Finalize the time

Tap your chosen slot, set a duration, add an optional note, and confirm. Everyone sees the final time.
