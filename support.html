Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a meeting?
Tap New Meeting in the app (or on the website), fill in a title and choose your preferred days or specific dates, then tap Create Meeting. You'll receive a shareable invite link and a private admin link.
What is the difference between the invite link and the admin link?
The invite link is for your participants — share it with anyone you want to invite. They can fill in their availability without creating an account. The admin link is private and gives you the ability to edit, finalize, or delete the meeting. Keep it safe — it is not stored in your account and will not be sent to you again.
Do participants need to create an account?
No. Anyone with the invite link can fill in their availability using just their name — no account or email address required.
How do I sign in?
MeetMe uses magic links — enter your email address and we'll send you a one-click sign-in link. No password required. You can also sign in with Google.
I lost my admin link. Can I recover it?
If you created the meeting while signed in to your account, you can access it from your Dashboard. If you created it anonymously (without an account), the admin link cannot be recovered — please create a new meeting.
How do I finalize a meeting time?
Open the meeting with your admin link, switch to Group View, and tap any time slot in the heatmap. You'll be able to set a duration and add an optional note before confirming. All participants will be able to see the finalized time.
Can I delete or edit a meeting?
Yes. Open the meeting with your admin link (or from your Dashboard if you have an account) and tap the trash icon to delete it. Editing the meeting title and dates is available from the same screen.
What data does MeetMe collect?
Please see our Privacy Policy for full details. In short, we collect only what is needed to run the service: your email address (if you sign in), your name, and the availability slots you choose to share.
How do I delete my account?
Go to the Profile tab in the app (or visit your profile on the website), scroll to Danger Zone, and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
